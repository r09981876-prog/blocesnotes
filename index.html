Privacy Policy

SwiftNote Chrome Extension — Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction
SwiftNote ("we", "our", "the extension") is a free Chrome browser extension that provides note-taking, drawing, image editing, and translation features. This Privacy Policy explains what data we handle and how we handle it. By using SwiftNote, you agree to this policy.
2. Data We Collect
None. SwiftNote does not collect, store on our servers, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics of any kind. We have no backend, no database, and no server that receives your data.
3. Local Storage
SwiftNote uses your browser's local storage API exclusively to save:

• Your notes — stored locally in your browser, never transmitted
• Theme preference — dark or light mode setting
• Language preferences — last used translation languages

All of this data remains entirely on your device and can be cleared at any time by removing the extension.
4. Third-Party Services
SwiftNote uses one third-party service:

Google Translate API (translate.googleapis.com) — used only when you explicitly click the "Translate" button. Only the text you type is sent to Google for translation. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No other data is shared with any third party.
5. Permissions Explained
storage — to save your notes and preferences locally in your browser.
activeTab — to navigate the current tab to our website when you click the extension icon (non-English users only).
clipboardWrite — to let you copy translated text to your clipboard with one click.
6. Children's Privacy
SwiftNote does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk to children's privacy.
7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
8. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the form below or via the contact section on this page.
